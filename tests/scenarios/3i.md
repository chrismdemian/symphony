# Phase 3I scenario — Worker Pipeline Progress through the launcher

## Goal

Drive the WorkerPanel via the production launcher (`runStart`) with a fake
RPC + fake Maestro and confirm that the new Phase 3I pipeline bar +
gerund stage label render correctly under a real poll cycle for workers
spanning multiple roles and lifecycle statuses.

## What this scenario asserts

1. Empty-state baseline (no workers, no `█` cells, no gerund labels).
2. After populating three workers across three different roles
   (researcher running / implementer running / reviewer completed),
   the next 1s poll cycle:
   - shows each worker's gerund label (`Researching`, `Implementing`,
     `Reviewing`).
   - shows 15 total `█` cells (3 workers × 5 cells per row).
   - keeps the feature intent text intact alongside the new column.
3. After flipping the implementer to `failed`, the next poll cycle:
   - shows the failed status glyph (`✗`).
   - still renders 15 cells (the bar persists for terminal workers).
   - the failed worker's row keeps its gerund label intact.

## What this scenario does NOT assert

ANSI cell colors. `vitest.scenarios.config.ts` does NOT load
`tests/setup.ts` so chalk's color level resolves to 0; per-cell
truecolor SGR escapes are absent from captured output. Color rules
are exhaustively covered by `tests/ui/panels/workers/PipelineBar.test.tsx`
(unit) and `pnpm visual:3i` + skeptical-subagent review (visual gate).

## Acceptance

`pnpm test:scenarios -- 3i.test.ts` exits 0.
